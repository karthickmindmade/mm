About MindMade

what we do

A Satisfied Customer is best for business

MindMade Technologies - the culmination of more than 15 years of dedication, sustained effort and a pinch of creativity in every endeavour! A team – fortified with ambition, zeal and a vision! MindMade Technologies is holistic IT Solutions Company specialised in delivering IT Outsourcing Services, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Services, Search Engine Marketing (SEM) Services, Content Management System (CMS) Based Website Development, E-Commerce Website Development, Website Design Services, Digital Branding Solutions and Mobile Website Development.

MISSION

To become a global organization by creating a platform for IT, and by delivering expert solutions, leveraged by the deployment of emerging technologies. (To make it short… “Being known to the World”)

VALUES

We are constantly improving our working environment, raising standards for life in MindMade and creating new values for our employees (family members). Our goal with every new solution coming out from MindMade is to bring new values, new benefits, for users worldwide.

Four Reasons to Work with Us

Uncompromising Quality

Quality is a relentless pursuit of craftsmanship that we put into every detail of our work. From the elegance of the concept to the precision of its execution, we are uncompromising. Quality is how we ensure that our work creates meaning and value for brands.

Keep it Transparent

We take pride in our honest approach to doing business. Accomplishing big things takes a lot of trust from our clients, partners and staffs, and we work hard to always be straightforward and clear in everything we do.

Experts over Executives

We believe that designing innovation takes peers from many disciplines. One must understand the constraints of design, technology and strategy to create an exceptional digital product. That is why we believe in allowing creative collaboration and exploration between these experts rather than mandating project plans to them.
